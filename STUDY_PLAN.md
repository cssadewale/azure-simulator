## 💡 Tips for Learning on an itel Vista Tab 30s

1. **Install the simulator as a PWA** — works offline, loads instantly
2. **Use Termux** for Azure CLI practice on Android
3. **Keep Microsoft Learn open** alongside the simulator — refer to the official docs as you explore
4. **Use night mode** (Dark Mode in simulator) for long study sessions to reduce eye strain
5. **Screenshot quiz explanations** for offline review
6. **Export your learning state** regularly (Settings → Export State) to keep a backup
